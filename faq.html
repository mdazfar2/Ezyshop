Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. Additionally, we offer cash on delivery in select areas.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be returned in their original packaging. To start a return, please contact our customer support.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we offer international shipping to over 100 countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location.

How do I contact customer support?

+

You can reach our customer support team by clicking the "Contact Us" button at the bottom of the page or emailing support@shop.com.
